Neon Forge — Web Design and Development

Who’s Behind Neon Forge?

Neon Forge is a small, focused creative studio built on the strength of two minds. One designer and one coder, who decided in mid 2025 to swap the confines of office life for the freedom to build things our own way. Between us, we bring together a blend of creative expertise and technical know how. BA [Hons] in Graphic Design on one side, and coding experience on the other.

That mix is at the heart of what we do. From crafting bold, memorable digital artwork to building websites that work as beautifully as they look, we cover every aspect of digital design and development with care, curiosity and a focus on getting the job done properly.

We’re not here to make grand mission statements, we'd rather set ourselves clear goals to deliver thoughtful, effective, and well built digital work for people and businesses who need it.

Our Services

We design and build websites that go beyond looks! Responsive, quick, and thoughtfully structured around how real people browse. From simple landing pages to full scale platforms, our aim is always the same - clean, reliable performance that feels effortless to use.

Identity and design systems sit at the heart of what we do. We create logos, typography, and visual languages that give brands clarity and cohesion. The foundations for everything from websites and campaigns to printed materials and packaging.

Digital artwork and campaign assets are another core part of our work. Whether it’s visuals for a launch, product imagery, or a complete set of marketing graphics, we build pieces that cut through noise and leave a lasting impression.

We also bring brands into the physical world. Posters, signage, packaging and other printed materials are designed to work seamlessly alongside your digital presence, extending your story across every touchpoint.

Behind the scenes, we focus on the details that help people find you. From SEO structure and semantic markup to speed optimisation, we fine tune sites so they perform well and rank naturally.

And when you need something more bespoke, we build lightweight web tools and simple apps that solve specific problems, automating processes, streamlining workflows, and making day to day tasks easier.
